How ChapSmart Works

A quick guide to our Lightning, Bitcoin on-chain, and USDT payment flows.

Lightning Payments ⚡
1️⃣ You enter the recipient’s mobile money details.
2️⃣ ChapSmart creates a Lightning invoice for your Satoshis.
3️⃣ You pay instantly using a Lightning wallet (e.g. Wallet of Satoshi).
4️⃣ ChapSmart converts to TZS and delivers to the recipient in seconds.
Bitcoin On-Chain
Prefer the main Bitcoin network?
1️⃣ Send BTC to the address we generate.
2️⃣ Wait for 1 confirmation.
3️⃣ Funds are converted and sent to the recipient’s mobile money account.
USDT Payments
Coming soon for stablecoin users: Send USDT (Tether) on supported networks, and the recipient receives the equivalent in Tanzanian Shillings. A great choice when you want to avoid Bitcoin price volatility.
Security & Best Practices
• Always double-check recipient numbers.
• Use a reputable Lightning or Bitcoin wallet.
• Keep your wallet seed phrase private and offline.
• Transactions are final once confirmed.
