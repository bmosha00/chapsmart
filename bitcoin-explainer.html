Bitcoin Explainer

Understand Bitcoin, Lightning, and Satoshis—and how to buy Bitcoin on exchanges like Binance and OKX before using ChapSmart.

What is Bitcoin?
Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency running on a global peer-to-peer network. It allows anyone to send money worldwide without banks or middlemen.
Satoshis (SATS)
One Bitcoin equals 100,000,000 Satoshis (“sats”). ChapSmart uses sats for flexible, low-fee payments—perfect for everyday transfers.
Lightning Network ⚡
A second-layer network on top of Bitcoin enabling instant, low-cost payments. ChapSmart leverages Lightning to deliver fast cross-border transactions.
Why ChapSmart Uses Bitcoin
• Borderless and censorship-resistant.
• Secure—protected by the strongest blockchain network.
• Inclusive—anyone with a smartphone can participate.
Getting Started
Download a Lightning-compatible wallet like Wallet of Satoshi or BlueWallet, fund it with BTC, and you’re ready to send or receive via ChapSmart.
Buy Bitcoin on Binance or OKX
Need Bitcoin first? You can purchase BTC directly on popular exchanges and then transfer to your Lightning wallet:

• Binance – Create an account, complete KYC, buy BTC with card or bank transfer, then withdraw to your wallet.
• OKX – Similar steps: register, verify, buy BTC, and withdraw to your Lightning-compatible wallet.

Once the funds reach your wallet, you can instantly use them on ChapSmart.
