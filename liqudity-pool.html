Frequently Asked Questions

Why USDT (Lightning) instead of Bitcoin?
Tether (USDT) on the Bitcoin Lightning Network provides stability with instant settlement and low fees, avoiding the high volatility of Bitcoin while maintaining the security benefits of the Bitcoin network.
How do liquidity pools work?
Liquidity pools allow you to deposit Tether (USDT) on the Bitcoin Lightning Network for a fixed 6-month period. Your funds are used to provide liquidity, and you earn competitive APY returns paid in Tether (USDT) on the Bitcoin Lightning Network throughout the term.
When can I withdraw my funds?
Funds are locked for the 6-month duration. Early withdrawals are not permitted to ensure stable returns for all participants.
How are APY returns calculated?
APY is calculated annually and distributed proportionally in Tether (USDT) on the Bitcoin Lightning Network based on your deposit amount and the pool's performance over the 6-month term.
What happens when my pool term ends?
At maturity, your principal plus earned yield in Tether (USDT) on the Bitcoin Lightning Network will be automatically returned to your wallet.
What are the benefits of using Lightning Network?
The Lightning Network enables instant transactions with minimal fees, making Tether (USDT) on the Bitcoin Lightning Network ideal for liquidity provision with fast settlements and cost-efficient operations.
